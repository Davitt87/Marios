The Beginning

In 1972 Mario Davitt served his first pizza to his first ever customer. Little did Mario know, this would be the beginning of a journey to establish a famous and sought after delicious recipe that would thrill the taste buds of many.

In 1980 Mario Davitt and after encountering Aoife one fateful afternoon in Naples they shortly married after. Mario and Aiofe moved to Ireland, where they opened their first International restuarant in the heart of Sligo town.

Mario's celebrates its 50th anniversary this year, and with the help of his family, and friends, it continues to be the sought after pizza restuarant in Ireland and Italy.

Restaurant Order

  1. 1972, 1st Mario's resturaunt is established - Naples, Italy
  2. 1978, 2nd Mario's restuarant opened - Naples, Italy
  3. 1979, 3rd Mario's restuarant opened - Naples, Italy
  4. 1981, 1st Mario's restuarant opened in Ireland - Sligo, Ireland
  5. 1985, 2nd Mario's restuarant opened in Ireland - Galway, Ireland
  6. 1987, 4th Mario's restuarant opened - Milan, Italy
  7. 1990, 3rd Mario's restuarant opened in Ireland - Dublin, Ireland
  8. 1996, 4th Mario's restuarant opened in Ireland - Cork, Ireland
  9. 2002, 1st Mario's restuarant opened in England - Manchester, England
  10. 2009, 5th Mario's restuarant opened in Ireland - Longford, Ireland
  11. 2015, 2nd Mario's restuarant opened in England - Liverpool, England
  12. 2022, 1st Mario's celebrates 50 years of business
